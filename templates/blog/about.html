About PhishGuard

Our Mission

To empower Zambians with the knowledge and skills to identify and defend against phishing attacks through interactive, AI-powered education.

The Problem

According to recent reports, 79% of Zambians have been targeted by phishing, smishing, vishing, and social engineering scams, with 9% falling victim to these attacks. The ZICTA National Cyber Risk Assessment Report identified phishing as one of the greatest perceived threat vectors in Zambia.

Our Solution

PhishGuard provides an interactive, AI-powered learning platform that includes:

  • Educational Modules - Comprehensive lessons on email phishing, SMS phishing (smishing), and social engineering
  • Realistic Simulations - Practice identifying phishing attempts in a safe environment
  • Pre/Post Assessments - Measure your improvement with before and after testing
  • Adaptive Learning - AI-powered recommendations based on your performance
  • Progress Tracking - Monitor your learning journey with detailed analytics

Why Phishing Education Matters

Phishing attacks are 100% preventable with proper education. By learning to recognize the warning signs, you can protect yourself, your family, and your organization from:

  • Financial losses
  • Identity theft
  • Data breaches
  • Malware infections

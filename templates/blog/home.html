What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where criminals try to trick you into revealing sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, or personal data.

  • Email Phishing: Fake emails pretending to be from trusted sources
  • Smishing: Fraudulent SMS text messages
  • Vishing: Voice phishing through phone calls
  • Social Engineering: Manipulation tactics to gain trust
Learn More

Warning Signs

  • Urgent requests for personal information
  • Suspicious sender email addresses
  • Poor grammar and spelling
  • Links that don't match the claimed destination
  • Unexpected attachments
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers
